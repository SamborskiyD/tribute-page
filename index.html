Time line of Winston Churchill`s life:

  • 1874 - born at his family's ancestral home, Blenheim Palace in Oxfordshire.
  • 1888 - aged 13, he narrowly passed the entrance exam for Harrow School.
  • 1893 - After two unsuccessful attempts to gain admittance to the Royal Military Academy, Sandhurst, he succeeded on his third.
  • 1900 - 26 years old for the first time became a member of the House of Commons
  • 1910 - At the age of 35, Churchill takes over as Home Secretary.
  • 1914 - 1918 - Took part in the first world war
  • 1921 - Churchill was appointed Colonial Secretary
  • 1929 - 1939 - Political isolation
  • 1940 - Churchill's appointment as prime minister
  • 1939 - 1945 Took part in the second world war
  • 1965 - Churchill died from a stroke.
